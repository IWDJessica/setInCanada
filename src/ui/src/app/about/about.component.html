Why London?


Known as the "Forest City," London, Ont., is a typical Canadian community in many ways. Located approximately two hours southwest of Toronto, the city is home to a major university and has a thriving medical research community. It attracts investors and business people as it is becoming the hub which connects big cities in Canada to America
