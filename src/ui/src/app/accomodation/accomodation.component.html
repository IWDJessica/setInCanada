Townhouse

Cost

Less expensive and more accessible.

Type and Ownership

Conjoined, detached or semi-detached units owned by individual homeowners.

Moder Features

Offer updated features like hardwood floors, granite countertops, and open concept.

Low-maintenance

Offer the spaciousness of a single family home along with the convenience of a condominium when it comes to upkeep.

Privacy and security

Townhouses have private outdoor areas. Close proximity to the neighbouring buildings offer more security.



Family House

Higher appreciation

Single family homes generally experience higher price appreciation, because the homeowner owns the land.

High Cost & Effort

Responsible for all of the maintenance and repairs

Square footage

Detached homes are usually larger and also offer larger yards, which can be used to gardening or outdoor entertaining.

Privacy

A house doesn’t share walls or common spaces as some condos and townhomes do, meaning you have more privacy.
